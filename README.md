## 📌 Project Summary

This project presents a **real-world, end-to-end data analytics workflow** using Uber ride booking data. The goal is to transform raw data into **actionable business insights** through structured data cleaning, SQL-based analysis, and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Understand ride demand patterns across time and categories
* Identify peak booking hours and high-traffic periods
* Analyze revenue and fare distribution
* Support data-driven operational and pricing decisions

---

## 🗂 Dataset Overview

**Primary Dataset:** `Uber_Booking_Dataset.csv`

Key attributes include:

* Booking date & time
* Pickup and drop-off information
* Trip distance and duration
* Fare amount and payment details
* Ride category / service type

📌 *All data was cleaned, validated, and standardized before analysis.*

## 🔍 Data Cleaning (Excel)

* Removed duplicates and invalid records
* Handled missing and inconsistent values
* Standardized date, time, and numeric formats
* Created derived columns for analysis readiness

This step ensured **high-quality, analysis-ready data** before SQL and visualization.

---

## 📊 SQL Analysis Performed

* Booking volume trends (daily, weekly, monthly)
* Peak demand hours analysis
* Revenue and fare distribution
* Trip distance and duration analysis
* Ride category performance comparison

All queries are documented in `Uber_Rides_Analysis.sql`.

---

## 📈 Power BI Dashboard Highlights

The dashboard delivers **quick, decision-focused insights**:

* KPI cards: Total Rides, Total Revenue, Avg Fare
* Time-series analysis of bookings
* Category-wise and demand-based breakdowns
* Interactive filters for deep exploration

📌 *Designed for both business stakeholders and analysts.*

---

## 💡 Key Insights & Business Impact

* Identified **peak demand windows** for optimal driver allocation
* Highlighted **high-revenue ride categories**
* Revealed pricing and distance patterns impacting revenue
* Enables **data-driven operational planning**
